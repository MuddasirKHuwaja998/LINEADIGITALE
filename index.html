How It Works

Four moves.
No templates.

Tap the + on any step to see what's inside.

  1. 01

    Discovery & Scope

    We learn your business, your clients, and where automation will actually save you time.

    • 60-minute call with the founder / operations lead to map your daily workflow
    • Clear scope document: what we build, what you own, what it costs
    • Timeline agreed upfront — weeks, not quarters
  2. 02

    Design & Training

    We build the UI, prepare your data, and train the AI on your actual business content.

    • Design mockups shared for approval before any coding starts
    • We gather your documents, FAQs, and product info for training
    • Models fine-tuned on your content — not generic internet data
  3. 03

    Launch & Integrate

    We ship it live, connect it to your existing tools, and train your team to use it.

    • Deployed on your domain or ours — you decide
    • Connected to WhatsApp, email, CRM, or whatever tools you already use
    • Team walkthrough + written handover so nothing is a black box
  4. 04

    Support & Improve

    We watch how it performs in the real world and refine the model and UI based on real usage.

    • Monthly check-ins to review usage, errors, and user feedback
    • Model retraining as your content grows and your business evolves
    • Direct line to us — no tickets, no bots, just WhatsApp or email
